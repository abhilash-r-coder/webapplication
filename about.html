Signature forgery detection

Every person has a unique signature used mainly for personal identification and verification of essential documents or legal transactions. There are two kinds of signature verification: static and dynamic. Static(offline) verification is verifying an electronic or paper signature after it has been made. In contrast, dynamic(online) verification occurs as a person creates their signature on a digital tablet or a similar device.

What are we trying to do?

developign an project which helps in identifying wether image is real or fake

context

There is a need for a system that can distinguish between genuine and forged signatures to avoid the chances of theft or fraud. Hence, we aim to create an intelligent model that gets trained with the real signature data sets and can detect a forged signature based on the available forged signature data sets using the Hog and CNN algorithm. The proposed system achieves an accuracy of 95.83% for forgery detection
